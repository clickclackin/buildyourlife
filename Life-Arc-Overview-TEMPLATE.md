## The Approach

**Core philosophy:** Fixed north star (1-year goals), flexible path (rolling 2-month window).

### Monthly Framework

Each month is structured as:

| Layer | Effort | What it is |
|---|---|---|
| **Survive** | 3/10 — floor only | 3 fixed weekly habits; non-negotiable but minimal |
| **Sustain** | ~5/10 — acceptable | Hardest thing from last month that needs locking in |
| **Grow (primary)** | ~7/10 — uncomfortable | 1 main growth target |
| **Grow (secondary)** | ~5/10 | 1 target in a different pillar |

**Survive goals** (weekly, resets each week — no streaks):
1. [e.g. Gym: minimum 2 sessions any given week]
2. [e.g. Diet: hit targets at least 4/7 days]
3. [e.g. Social: 1 social exposure any given week]

**Crash mode** (self-activated, no formal process):
- Drop to 2 of 3 survive goals for the rest of that week
- Grow and Sustain expectations pause — no guilt, no catch-up
- Weekly check-in can be skipped; monthly reset still happens

### Rollover Logic

- **Primary grow not completed:** Rolls forward as next month's Sustain. A new primary grow is set in a different pillar.
- **Primary grow fails 2+ consecutive months:** Monthly reset examines root cause — goal too big, wrong timing, or low genuine motivation.
- **Partial progress is progress.** The Progress Log records partial completion. Monthly reset frames incomplete targets as "continuing," not "failed."
